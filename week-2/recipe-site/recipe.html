Best Banana Bread Recipe (Moist!)

If you've ever woken up and thought to yourself, "I could use some amazing banana bread that is super moist and will make me the happiest person in the world!" Then this recipe is for you! Follow along to make the best banana bread! :)

Ingredients:

Wet Ingredients

  • Butter 1/2 cup
  • Sugar Between 1/2 and 1 cup
  • 2 Eggs
  • Vanilla Somewhere between 1/2 to 2 teaspoons
  • Sour cream 1/4 to 1/2 cup
  • Banana 2-6 bananas

Dry Ingredients

  • Flour Between 1 and 1/2 to 2 cups
  • Baking soda 1 teaspoon
  • Salt 1/4 teaspoon

Directions:

  1. Beat the butter and sugar. Get it nice and creamy.
  2. Add in eggs, vanilla, mashed banana.
  3. Add in the dry ingredients.
  4. Fold in the sour cream, fold in some nuts if you’re using them, and voila!
  5. Bake it up for an hour at 350 degrees and you’re done.

About this recipe!

This is my go-to recipe for banana bread! I've made it for my fiance, bridal showers, and as a breakfast option. I would 100% recommend!
