Nirvana

Nirvana was an American rock band formed in Aberdeen, Washington. 195 miles from Spokane, WA. Founded by lead singer and guitarist Kurt Cobain and bassist Krist Novoselic, the band went through a succession of drummers, most notably Chad Channing, before recruiting Dave Grohl in 1990.
